Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our vehicles and services

What financing options do you offer?

We offer a variety of financing options including bank financing, in-house financing, and lease options. Our financial experts will work with you to find the best solution based on your credit profile and budget.

Do you offer vehicle maintenance services?

Yes, we have a state-of-the-art service center staffed with factory-trained technicians. We offer maintenance packages for all vehicles purchased from us, including routine servicing, repairs, and genuine parts replacement.

Can I schedule a test drive?

Absolutely! You can schedule a test drive by calling us, using the contact form, or clicking the "Schedule Test Drive" button on our website. We'll arrange a convenient time for you to experience your desired vehicle.

Do you offer vehicle delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery services anywhere in Nigeria. Delivery fees vary based on your location. We also provide international shipping for clients outside Nigeria.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy on all vehicles, subject to certain conditions. The vehicle must be in the same condition as when purchased, with no more than 500 kilometers added. Some exceptions may apply for custom-ordered vehicles.
