Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our financing process

What credit score do I need to qualify for financing?
We work with a wide range of credit profiles. While ideal rates are available for scores of 700 and above, we have options for various credit situations. Submit an application, and our finance team will find the best solution for you.
How long does the approval process take?
In many cases, we can provide pre-approval within hours of receiving your completed application. The entire process from application to funding typically takes 1-2 business days.
What documents do I need to apply for financing?
Typically, you'll need proof of income (recent pay stubs or tax returns), proof of residence (utility bill or lease agreement), a valid driver's license, and insurance information. Our finance team will provide a complete list based on your specific situation.
Can I finance a vehicle with a trade-in?
Absolutely! We welcome trade-ins and can incorporate your vehicle's value into the financing arrangement. Our team will appraise your current vehicle to determine its value toward your new purchase.
Are there any prepayment penalties?
No, all our financing options allow you to pay off your loan early without any prepayment penalties. This gives you the flexibility to save on interest costs if you're able to pay ahead of schedule.
